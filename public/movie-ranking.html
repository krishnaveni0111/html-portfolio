The Best Movies According to Krishna

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avatar


This is my favourite science fiction movie. This film was shot using a new type of 3D camera that was specifically designed for th film,giving it a more immersive and realistic feel.

Interstellar

Really cool sci-fi movie.The films visual effects,especially the black hole are impressive

The Pursuit of Happyness

The Pursuit of Happyness can be summarised in one word: Grit! Having a dream is not enough, you need the courage and resolve to pursue it.
